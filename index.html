About Me

I am an engineer very passionate about AI, code and robotics. My journey started as a Mechanical Engineer from IIT Bombay, where I worked to build an electric car to participate in Formula Student UK Competition. From there, I moved on to explore robotics and did my master's thesis in autonomous drones. I worked at Jaguar Land Rover working on electric motors for their electric vehicles for 2 years.

Fascinated by AI in healthcare, I moved to Qure.ai. I first started working on Qure.ai's flagship product qXR which is AI fast reporting in Chest X-Rays. Currently, I am working on building Qure.ai's Stroke Suite which is an AI powered solution for early triaging and timely treatment of stroke patients.

I am extremely fascinated by drones. In my free time, I build and race FPV drones. I love to tell stories with my drone videos as you will see below.

    Passion for FPV Drones

    I am extremely passionate about FPV drones. I love their speed, agility and the rush of adrenaline that they give. I have built 4 drones so far which I use for racing and aerial photography. I got into FPV since 2021 and haven't stopped since then.

    My collection of custom-built FPV drones ready for action

    Exhilarating moments from an FPV drone race

    I get this question a lot about how do I build these drones. It generally comprises of buying each component, putting it together, soldering components, flashing the firmware and setting up the settings. After building the drone, the harder part is learning to fly and control them. These are very fast and agile and I have sent 100s of hours on a simulator to get better at them. I have participated in multiple races too. In the 2022 IDRL race held at IIT Delhi, I was able to secure 3rd position.

    When you fly drones, very far away from you, it is very stressful. You have to learn to deal with the risk, fly the drone and get it back. You have to be ready for it to crash and only then you can improve as a great pilot. All in all, it is super fun to fly them. When you record your video, edit it, add a song on it, it all comes together and gives an amazing memory. When I travel, I always make it a point to make cinematic videos of the place I am visiting.
